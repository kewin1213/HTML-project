The Bulls

About them

The 1995-96 Bulls were undoubtedly the most successful
team in the NBA of that era.They had an 87.8 win % throughout
a whole season. They went 72(wins) to 10 (losses).



The main players of the team were Michael Jordan (23),
Scottie Pippen (33) and Denis Rodman (91). They were the
unstoppable trio. Michael Jordan the legend and leader in
all aspects (at the time), Pippen the most well rounded player
and amazing teammate and Rodman, the all time leader in
rebounds and defensive plaayer of the year on many occasions,
were the deadliest around.



They managed to amass a total of 6 championships in the
years 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998.

The other years, Jordan wasn't on the team.
